Digital Printing

Digital Print allows cost effective printing of short runs and highly personalized communications.

The quality of digital print is excellent and allows you great flexibility. Digital printing differs from traditional offset in that the ink does not absorb into the paper, but forms a layer on the surface. It requires less waste in terms of chemicals and paper used during set up. This cost-effective method is great for those jobs that require smaller quantities and a quick turnaround. Simply upload your files and let our highly trained staff take care of the rest. Contact our sales team to discuss your project or to request samples.
