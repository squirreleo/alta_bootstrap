Printing Responsibly

Did you know Alta is FSC-certified? The Forest Stewardship Council (FSC®) standards represent the world's strongest system for guiding forest management toward sustainable outcomes. Your purchase of FSC-certified paper and print products contributes to conservation, responsible management, and community level benefits for people near the forests that provide your paper. Visit us.fsc.org for more information about this global project. Contact our sales team to request the FSC® logo on your next print product.

Resources and FAQs

How do I send files for my project?

There are several ways to send a file:

  • Upload the files to our FTP Site.
  • Share a Dropbox folder with your customer service representative.
  • Email the files (be aware that large files may take extra time and/or fail to deliver).
  • Contact your customer service representative to schedule a courier pickup.
  • Drop off a disc or flash drive. Find us here.

How much room do I need on a mailpiece for the address?

Letter-size Mail

  • Description:between 5" long x 3.5" high and 11.5" long x 6.125" high
  • Aspect Ratio: Length ÷ Height should be between 1.3 and 2.5
  • No-print Area: bottom 5/8" will be used by USPS
  • Address Area: 3.75" long x 2" high in the bottom 2.75" of the piece

Click template to enlarge

Flat-size Mail

  • Description:between 5" long x 3.5" high and 11.5" long x 6.125" high
  • Aspect Ratio: Length ÷ Height should be between 1.3 and 2.5
  • No-print Area: bottom 5/8" will be used by USPS
  • Address Area (horizontal orientation): 3.75" long x 2" high located fully on right or left side of piece
  • Address Area (vertical orientation): 3.75" long x 2" high located fully on top half of piece

What is the difference between presort standard, nonprofit and first class mailings?

First Class Presort

  • Minimum Quantity: 500
  • Delivery Time: highest priority
  • Postage Discount: about 20% off of retail
  • Price Break: over 1 ounce

Standard Presort

  • Minimum Quantity: 200
  • Delivery Time: lowest priority
  • Postage Discount: about 40% off of retail
  • Price Break: over 3.3 ounces

Nonprofit

  • Minimum Quantity: 200
  • Delivery Time: same as standard presort
  • Postage Discount: about 70% off of retail
  • Price Break: over 3.3 ounces

Why do I have to review proofs of my project?

The proofing process is very important to the success of your project. We go to great lengths to proof every job for accuracy; however, mistakes can happen, so read your proof carefully.

Unless alternative arrangements are made with your CSR—or your job is an exact reprint and we have a previous sample—you can expect to review a PDF (soft) proof and a hard proof when raw files are supplied. The soft proof will generally be skipped if PDF files are submitted.

By emailing your approval or by signing your proof envelope, you are authorizing Alta to print your job as shown on your proof. Understand that by giving your approval any errors not detected are your sole responsibility and that you are responsible for full payment.
